## Part 2: The Brainstorm & ChatGPT Prompt (10:10 AM – 10:35 AM)

*(Screen share your browser. Open ChatGPT.)*

**Script:**

> *"Before we generate music, we need a **concept**. AI doesn't create magic from nothing—it needs a great prompt.*
>
> *Ask yourself: Who is this song for?*
> *- Are you creating a jingle for a restaurant?*
> *- A podcast intro for a tech show?*
> *- A motivational anthem for students?*
>
> *Let me show you how to write a killer prompt for ChatGPT.*
>
> *Here is the formula:*
> **"[Genre] + [Topic/Theme] + [Target Audience] + [Tone/Emotion]"**
>
> *Watch me do this:*
>
> **Step 1:** I type this into ChatGPT:
> *"Write a 30-second catchy jingle for a Lagos-based bakery called 'Golden Crust.' Genre: Afrobeat. Tone: Happy, energetic, and welcoming. Target audience: Young professionals in Lagos. Use both English and small Yoruba."*
>
> **Step 2:** ChatGPT generates the lyrics.
> *(Wait 10 seconds for the output to appear on screen.)*
>
> *Look at that. In 10 seconds, we have a full song structure—Verse, Chorus, Bridge—that sounds like a real radio ad.*
>
> *Now, let's do a podcast script example:*
>
> *"Write a 60-second intro script for a podcast called 'The Tech Hustle.' Tone: energetic, professional, and futuristic. Target audience: Nigerian entrepreneurs."*
>
> *(Show the output.)*
>
> *Now you have the blueprint. You don't need to be a writer. You just need to know what to ask for."*
