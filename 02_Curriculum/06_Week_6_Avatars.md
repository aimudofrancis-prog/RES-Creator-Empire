# WEEK 6: AI Influencer Avatars

**Tool:** D-ID or HeyGen
**Learning Objective:** Students will learn how to create talking avatars using a single photo and a voiceover.

---

## 1. Tool Setup

- **D-ID:**
  1. Go to `d-id.com`.
  2. Sign up with your email.
  3. Upload a photo and an audio file.

- **HeyGen:**
  1. Go to `heygen.com`.
  2. Sign up and explore the interface.

---

## 2. How It Works

1. Upload a photo of a person (or a character).
2. Upload an audio file (or record your voice).
3. AI animates the face to match the audio.
4. Download the video.

---

## 3. Step-by-Step Exercise (Live Class)

1. Open D-ID or HeyGen.
2. Upload a photo of a person.
3. Record a 30-second voiceover: *"Hi, I'm an AI influencer. I can help you grow your business."*
4. Press Generate.
5. Wait 1 minute.
6. Download the video.

---

## 4. Assignment (Homework)

**Task:** Create a **30-second "faceless" Instagram Reel** using an avatar.

- Use a photo of a character or a generic face.
- Record a voiceover about a topic you love.
- The avatar must speak the script.

**Submit:**
1. The video.
2. Your name.

**Deadline:** Friday, 11:59 PM WAT.

---
