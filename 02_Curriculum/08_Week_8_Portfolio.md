# WEEK 8: Portfolio Building + Client Pitching

**Learning Objective:** Students will learn how to create a professional 1-page portfolio and pitch their services to real businesses.

---

## 1. The 1-Page "OneSheet" Portfolio

A OneSheet has 5 sections:

| Section | Content |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Header** | Your name, contact info (email, WhatsApp). |
| **Skills** | AI Image Generation, Music, Video, Copywriting, Photo Editing, Avatars. |
| **Best Projects** | 3 images of your best work (with a short description). |
| **Testimonial** | A fake or real quote from a teacher or client. |
| **Call to Action** | "Hire me for your next project. DM me." |

---

## 2. Step-by-Step Exercise (Live Class)

1. Open Canva.
2. Select "Portfolio" template.
3. Add your header: Name, Email, WhatsApp.
4. Add your skills: AI Image Gen, Video, Copywriting, etc.
5. Upload 3 best projects from Weeks 1–7.
6. Add a testimonial.
7. Add a Call to Action.
8. Download as PDF.

---

## 3. The Cold Email Pitch

**Script:**
> *"Dear [Business Name],*
> 
> *My name is [Your Name]. I am an AI content creator trained by RES Creators. I specialize in generating images, videos, and copy for businesses.*
> 
> *I attached my portfolio below. I would love to help you with your social media content or product images.*
> 
> *Let me know if you are open to a quick chat.*
> 
> *Warm regards,*
> *[Your Name]"*

---

## 4. Assignment (Homework)

**Task:** Create your 1-page portfolio and send a cold email or DM to **one real local business**.

- Attach your portfolio.
- Use the script above.
- Send via email or WhatsApp.

**Submit:**
1. A screenshot of the email or DM you sent.
2. Your name.
