# WEEK 2: AI Music & Sound Design

**Tool:** Suno AI or Udio
**Learning Objective:** Students will learn how to generate custom background scores and jingles for businesses.

---

## 1. Tool Setup

- **Suno AI:**
  1. Go to `suno.com`.
  2. Sign up with your email (free credits available).
  3. Click "Create" to start generating music.

- **Udio:**
  1. Go to `udio.com`.
  2. Sign up and explore the interface.

---

## 2. How to Write a Music Prompt

A good music prompt includes:

| Part | Example |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Genre** | Afrobeat, Pop, Classical, Lo-fi |
| **Mood** | Happy, Relaxing, Intense, Sad |
| **Instrumentation** | Piano, Drums, Guitar, Electronic |
| **Length** | 15 seconds, 1 minute, 3 minutes |

**Formula:**
> *"[Genre] music, [mood] mood, [instrumentation], [length]"*

---

## 3. Step-by-Step Exercise (Live Class)

1. Open Suno AI or Udio.
2. Type this prompt:
   > *"Upbeat Afrobeat instrumental, happy mood, talking drums and bass, 30 seconds."*
3. Press Generate.
4. Wait 20 seconds.
5. Listen and download.
