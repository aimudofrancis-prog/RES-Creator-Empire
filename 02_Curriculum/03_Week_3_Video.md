# WEEK 3: AI Video Creation

**Tool:** RunwayML or Pika Labs
**Learning Objective:** Students will learn how to turn text and images into short AI-generated videos.

---

## 1. Tool Setup

- **RunwayML:**
  1. Go to `runwayml.com`.
  2. Sign up with your email.
  3. Select "Gen-2" for text-to-video generation.

- **Pika Labs:**
  1. Go to `pika.art`.
  2. Sign up and explore the interface.

---

## 2. Text-to-Video Prompt Formula

A video prompt needs motion and action:

| Part | Example |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Subject** | A robot dancing |
| **Action** | Breaking dance moves |
| **Environment** | Cyberpunk city street |
| **Quality** | Cinematic, 4K |

**Formula:**
> *"[Subject] [action], [environment], [quality]"*

---

## 3. Step-by-Step Exercise (Live Class)

1. Open RunwayML or Pika Labs.
2. Type this prompt:
   > *"A robot dancing breaking dance moves, cyberpunk city street, cinematic lighting, 4K."*
3. Press Generate.
4. Wait 30–60 seconds.
5. Download the video.

---

## 4. Assignment (Homework)

**Task:** Take the 3D character you created in Week 1, and turn it into a **5-second dancing video**.

- Use RunwayML to animate it.
- Make it fun and energetic.

**Submit:**
